<original_problem_statement>
Build a cross-platform, offline-first application for a Farmer Producer Company (FPO) to manage inventory, sales, procurement, and billing across multiple outlets.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
- **User Roles:** Admin (full access) and Agent (outlet-level access).
- **Authentication:** Secure login. Admin creates outlets and agent users.
- **Inventory Management:** Track stock (opening, received, sold, damaged) per product and outlet. Admin has central visibility and can allocate stock.
- **Sales &amp; Billing:** Agents can record sales (cash, online, credit, partial). Bills are generated automatically (printable PDF). Offline billing with sync is required.
- **Customer Management:** Create customer profiles and maintain a ledger for sales, credit, and payments.
- **Farmer Procurement:** Create farmer profiles, record produce purchases, and maintain a farmer ledger for payments.
- **Dashboard &amp; Reports (Admin):** View daily/monthly sales, stock overview, and outlet performance. Generate and export reports (Sales, Stock, Customer, Farmer) to Excel.
- **Technical:** PWA for Android and desktop browsers, offline-first for mobile, bilingual (Hindi/English).
- **Initial Product list:** Agnistra (Litre), Sanjeevini Compost (kg), Multi seed Extract (Litre), Jeevamrit (Litre), Paddy Seeds.
</original_problem_statement>
